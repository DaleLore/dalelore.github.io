    Audiovisual Preservation EXchange

    • APEX 2018: CTAV (06/2018)
      Collaborated with Centro Técnico do Audiovisual (CTAV) film archives in Rio de Janeiro, Brasil. Inventoried over 500 items of various media formats (DCPs, ¼” audio reels, audio cassettes, 8mm film, VHS, Betacams, u-matics). Processed and cleaned 8mm film collection and 16mm film collections. Modified a NAGRA audio machine to digitize oral histories of a presidential election.

    • APEX 2016: Biblioteca Nacional and Señal 3 de la Victoria (05/2016)
      Collaborated with two organizations in Santiago, Chile - the Biblioteca Nacional and Señal 3, a community TV station, on their audiovisual collections. Spliced leader onto 100 films. Inventoried over 100 films for a home movie film collection. Built a telecine to digitize films for access copies. Inventoried over 200 video assets for Señal 3.

    • APEX 2015: TV Pública (06/2015)
      Coordinated with Museo del Cine and TV Pública to organize an audiovisual preservation exchange with NYU. Organized over 15 participants for a two week exchange in Buenos Aires. Inventoried and cleaned TV Pública’s two-inch quad collection of broadcasts of the 1980s.

    • APEX 2014: Fundación de Arte Contemporáneo (FAC) (06/2014)
      Collaborated with Fundación de Arte Contemporaneo (FAC) in film preservation. Learned the process and workflow of optical printing. Installed a performative film exhibition at AJI.

    • Museu do Arte Moderna (06/2014 - 09/2014)
      Worked as an Archival Film Specialist at the Cinemateca du MAM. Inspected, repaired, and cleaned over 200 35mm film prints for long-term storage. Translated film inspection sheet from Portuguese to English. Made recommendations that balanced best practices for audiovisual archiving with the limitations of the resources available to the Museu de Arte Moderna.
